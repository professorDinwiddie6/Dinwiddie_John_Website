Simple Sally's Banana Cream Pie

Ingredients

  • Great Value pie crust
  • 1 box instant banana cream pudding (3.4oz)
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 cup Cool Whip
  • 3 Bananas

Instructions

  1. Slice 1 banana and layer bottom of crust
  2. Combine milk and pudding mix and whisk until thickened.
  3. Fold in 2/3 of Cool Whip.
  4. Mix 1 banana, sliced into filling.
  5. Add filling to pie crust and chill for 2 hours (for best results).
  6. Slice 1 banana and layer top of pie.
  7. Add remaining Cool Whip to top of pie and sprinkle graham cracker crumbs.
